Cook once, eat twice. Four dinners. Zero waste. Two people.

Week 01

The Classics, Done Well

Monday 30 min
🍲
Chicken thigh & chickpea stew
Bone-in thighs braised with canned tomatoes, chickpeas, garlic, onion, smoked paprika and cumin. Serve over rice or with crusty bread.
☀️ Tuesday lunch
Reheat as-is or spoon into a wrap with a handful of spinach. Flavours deepen overnight.
Tuesday 25 min
🍝
Sausage & tomato pasta
Italian sausage broken into chunks, sautéed with garlic and onion, simmered in canned tomatoes. Toss with rigatoni, finish with parmesan.
☀️ Wednesday lunch
Reheat with a splash of water, or eat room-temp with lemon and fresh spinach stirred in.
Wednesday 25 min
🥩
Beef & veggie stir-fry over rice
Thinly sliced flank steak stir-fried with garlic, onion, bell pepper and spinach in a soy-garlic sauce. One pan, served over steamed rice.
☀️ Friday lunch
Pack over rice. Add a squeeze of lemon and sesame oil if you have it. Great cold too.

Week 02

A Little More Adventure

Monday 30 min
🫘
Smoky black bean & sweet potato tacos
Black beans and roasted sweet potato with cumin, chipotle, garlic and lime. Serve in warm tortillas with sliced avocado and a quick cabbage slaw.
☀️ Tuesday lunch
Reheat the filling and wrap it up again, or pile over rice with a dollop of sour cream.
Tuesday 25 min
🍜
Coconut curry noodle soup
Coconut milk, red curry paste, garlic, ginger and chicken broth with rice noodles and shredded rotisserie chicken. Topped with lime and fresh coriander.
☀️ Wednesday lunch
Store broth and noodles separately if possible. Reheat broth, add fresh noodles or yesterday's — still great.
Wednesday 25 min
🍳
Shakshuka with feta & crusty bread
Eggs poached in a spiced tomato and pepper sauce with cumin, paprika and garlic. Crumble feta on top. Serve straight from the pan with bread.
☀️ Thursday lunch
Reheat the sauce gently and crack in fresh eggs — takes 8 minutes. Or just eat the sauce cold over bread.
