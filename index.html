Plan 01 · Mediterranean

Rotisserie
Chicken Week

One store-bought rotisserie chicken covers four dinners across three flavor profiles. Simmer the carcass while you eat night one — free stock for night three.

🍗
ANCHOR
Rotisserie chicken Farro or barley Canned chickpeas
4 Nights
1
Night One
Harissa chicken & farro bowl with roasted zucchini, feta, cucumber
Use breast meat. Cook the full farro pot tonight — it feeds night two as well.
  • Strip the whole chicken, separate white & dark meat
  • Rinse 1.5 cups farro, bring to boil with salted water
  • Slice zucchini into half-moons, toss with oil & salt
  • Roast zucchini at 425°F until caramelized, ~20 min
  • Dice cucumber, crumble feta, set aside
  • Simmer carcass in water with onion overnight for stock
2
Night Two
Chicken & chickpea skillet with tomatoes, olives, lemon, wilted spinach ↻ farro
Use thigh and leg meat. Serve over leftover farro reheated with a splash of stock.
  • Drain and rinse chickpeas, pat dry
  • Halve olives, mince 3 garlic cloves
  • Sauté garlic, add tomatoes + chickpeas, cook 5 min
  • Add dark meat chicken, olives, squeeze of lemon
  • Wilt spinach in last 2 minutes
  • Reheat farro with a splash of stock in a small pan
3
Night Three
Chicken noodle soup from carcass stock — carrots, celery, egg noodles
Simmer the carcass overnight after night one. Free, rich stock ready by morning.
  • Strain the carcass stock, discard solids
  • Peel and slice carrots into coins
  • Slice celery into thin half-moons
  • Dice half an onion, sauté in pot until soft
  • Add stock + veg, simmer 10 min
  • Add egg noodles, cook until tender, season well
4
Night Four
Sheet pan chicken sausage with roasted potatoes and broccolini
One link per person. Fresh protein closes the week on a completely different note.
  • Halve baby potatoes, toss with oil, salt, garlic powder
  • Break broccolini into florets, trim woody ends
  • Slice sausage links on the bias into thick coins
  • Spread everything on sheet pan, don't crowd it
  • Roast at 425°F for 25 min, flip halfway
Prep Notes + Shopping
✦ Prep Notes
Peel and slice 3 carrots into coins, slice 3 celery stalks into half-moons
Slice zucchini into ½-inch half-moons, toss with oil and salt before roasting
Dice cucumber into small cubes, keep separate until plating
Pull chicken meat off the bone in large chunks — don't shred it too fine
Shopping List
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Chicken sausage (4 links)
  • Farro or pearl barley
  • Canned chickpeas
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Egg noodles
  • Zucchini + broccolini
  • Carrots, celery, onion
  • Baby or Yukon potatoes
  • Spinach
  • Feta crumbles
  • Kalamata olives
  • Harissa paste
  • Garlic + cucumber
Plan 02 · Tuscan

White Bean
Week

Dried cannellini beans cooked from scratch anchor all four nights. One pot of stew on night one becomes pasta e fagioli by night two — zero extra work.

🫘
ANCHOR
Dried cannellini beans Italian or andouille sausage Parmesan rind
4 Nights
1
Night One
White bean & sausage stew with kale, rosemary, parmesan rind, crusty bread
Make a big pot — this is also the base for night 2. The rind is non-negotiable.
  • Drain soaked beans, rinse well
  • Dice onion, celery, and carrot into small pieces
  • Slice sausage into rounds, brown in pot first
  • Sauté veg in sausage fat, add garlic + rosemary
  • Strip kale leaves from stems, tear into pieces
  • Add beans, tomatoes, parmesan rind + water, simmer 45 min
2
Night Two
Pasta e fagioli ↻ stew
Thin leftover stew with pasta water. Stir in ditalini or small shells. Done in 12 minutes.
  • Bring salted water to a boil for the pasta
  • Reheat stew over medium, adding a ladle of pasta water
  • Cook ditalini 2 min less than package says
  • Scoop pasta directly into stew with a slotted spoon
  • Finish with good olive oil and black pepper
3
Night Three
Skillet sausage with white beans, fennel, garlic, white wine pan sauce
Fresh cook tonight. The fennel completely shifts the flavor direction from the stew nights.
  • Trim fennel, halve and slice thin — save the fronds
  • Slice sausage links lengthwise, brown cut-side down
  • Remove sausage, sauté fennel in same pan until soft
  • Add garlic, deglaze with white wine, scrape up all the bits
  • Add beans + sausage back in, cook 5 more minutes
  • Top with fennel fronds and a drizzle of olive oil
4
Night Four
Fried eggs over white bean purée with sautéed greens & sausage crumbles ↻ beans
Mash beans with olive oil and lemon. 20 minutes, incredibly filling, dead simple.
  • Warm beans in a pan with olive oil and 1 garlic clove
  • Mash roughly with a fork — chunky is better than smooth
  • Season with lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes
  • Sauté greens in a separate pan until wilted
  • Crumble any remaining sausage, crisp in the same pan
  • Fry eggs to your liking, lay over the purée
Prep Notes + Shopping
✦ Prep Notes
Drain the soaked beans and rinse under cold water until it runs clear
Dice onion, 2 carrots, and 2 celery stalks into a fine, even mince
Strip kale leaves off their stems, tear into palm-sized pieces
Trim fennel bulb, halve it, then slice very thin crosswise — save the green fronds
Shopping List
  • Dried cannellini beans (1 lb)
  • Italian or andouille sausage
  • Ditalini or small pasta
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Eggs (6-pack)
  • Crusty bread (1 loaf)
  • Kale or Swiss chard
  • Fennel bulb
  • Onion, garlic, celery, carrot
  • Parmesan rind or wedge
  • Dry white wine
  • Fresh or dried rosemary
  • Leafy greens (night 4)
  • Good olive oil
Plan 03 · Global

Ground Beef
& Lentil Week

1 lb of beef stretched 50/50 with lentils spans Mexican, Italian, and Middle Eastern flavors. Same cooked base, three completely different spice identities.

🥩
ANCHOR
Ground beef (1 lb) Green or French lentils Eggs
4 Nights
1
Night One
Beef & lentil picadillo tacos with pickled jalapeño, cotija, lime crema
Brown ALL the beef tonight. Use half the lentil batch with cumin, oregano, cinnamon.
  • Cook 1.5 cups lentils in salted water until just tender
  • Brown all 1 lb beef in a wide pan, break it up finely
  • Season half the beef with cumin, oregano, cinnamon, chili
  • Stir in half the lentils, cook together 5 minutes
  • Mix sour cream with lime zest + juice for crema
  • Warm tortillas directly over a gas flame or dry skillet
2
Night Two
Beef-lentil Bolognese over pappardelle or rigatoni ↻ beef
Same meat base, totally different world — bay, thyme, tomato paste, splash of milk.
  • Dice half an onion and 2 carrots very small
  • Sauté veg in olive oil until soft, ~8 minutes
  • Add reserved beef, stir in tomato paste, cook 2 min
  • Add whole tomatoes, bay leaf, thyme, splash of milk
  • Simmer 20 min low while pasta water comes to boil
  • Cook pasta, finish in sauce with pasta water
3
Night Three
Mujaddara with deeply caramelized onions, yogurt, warm spices ↻ lentils
The beef takes a night off. Caramelizing the onions is not optional — that IS the dish.
  • Slice 3 onions into thin half-moons
  • Cook onions in olive oil on medium-low for 35–40 min, stir often
  • Cook rice in salted water, fluff with fork
  • Warm lentils, season with cumin, coriander, cinnamon
  • Mix yogurt with a pinch of cumin and salt
  • Layer rice, lentils, caramelized onions in a bowl
4
Night Four
Shakshuka with lentils — eggs poached in spiced tomato-lentil sauce ↻ lentils
25-minute dinner. Serve with pita or crusty bread. The lentils make it properly filling.
  • Dice half an onion and 1 red pepper if you have one
  • Sauté onion, add cumin + smoked paprika, bloom 1 min
  • Add crushed tomatoes and remaining lentils, simmer 10 min
  • Make 4 wells in the sauce with the back of a spoon
  • Crack an egg into each well, cover and cook 5–7 min
  • Top with feta crumbles and fresh herbs if you have them
Prep Notes + Shopping
✦ Prep Notes
Slice 3 large onions into thin half-moons — uniform thickness so they cook evenly
Peel and dice 2 carrots very small, smaller than your pinky nail
Mince 4 garlic cloves, keep separate from the onions
Measure and pre-mix your spices for nights 1 and 2 into two small bowls
Shopping List
  • Ground beef 80/20 (1 lb)
  • Green or French lentils
  • Eggs (6-pack)
  • Pappardelle or rigatoni
  • Corn tortillas
  • Long-grain white rice
  • Canned whole tomatoes (×2)
  • Onions (3–4) + garlic
  • Pickled jalapeños
  • Cotija or feta
  • Sour cream or yogurt
  • Tomato paste
  • Cumin + smoked paprika
  • Cinnamon + bay leaves
